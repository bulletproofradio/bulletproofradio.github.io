Adam Clash

Adam Clash is the guitarist and lead singer of Bulletproof Radio. Born in Seattle, Washington, Adam was heavily influenced by grunge music from the early '90s. His favorite band growing up was Nirvana. In fact, the first song Adam learned to play on the guitar was "Smells Like Teen Spirit" by Nirvana. Lately, Adam is interested in acoustic rock and plans to release a solo project of folk songs.
